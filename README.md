# HR Dashboard Project

This project showcases my proficiency in data analysis and visualization using Power BI, with a focus on creating an intuitive and informative HR dashboard. Leveraging data from Excel files, I designed and developed a comprehensive dashboard to provide insights into various HR metrics, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions and strategic workforce planning.

## Project Objectives:

The primary objectives of this project were to:

1. **Data Preparation and Cleaning**:
   - Utilize Power Query in Power BI to clean and prepare the HR data from Excel files for analysis.
   - Standardize data formats, handle missing values, and ensure data integrity for accurate visualization.

2. **Dashboard Creation**:
   - Develop an interactive and user-friendly dashboard in Power BI to visualize key HR metrics.
   - Incorporate intuitive visuals and interactive elements to facilitate easy exploration of the data.

3. **Key Metrics Analysis**:
   - Analyze various HR parameters to extract actionable insights, including:
     - Average salary across different job roles.
     - Distribution of employees by job title.
     - Gender breakdown by male and female employees.
     - Identification of top and lowest earners in each job role.
     - Age spread of staff by gender.
     - Overall headcount of the organization.

## Dashboard Highlights:

- **Average Salary**: Visual representation of the average salary across different job roles, providing insights into compensation trends within the organization.
  
- **Employee Distribution**: Bar chart displaying the number of employees in each job role, aiding in workforce planning and resource allocation.

- **Gender Breakdown**: Pie chart showcasing the gender distribution among employees, highlighting diversity and inclusion efforts within the organization.

- **Top and Lowest Earners**: Table visualization identifying the top earners and lowest earners in each job role, facilitating salary benchmarking and performance evaluation.

- **Age Spread**: Histogram depicting the age distribution of staff by gender, enabling analysis of workforce demographics and succession planning.

- **Headcount**: Card visualization displaying the total headcount of the organization, providing an overview of workforce size and growth trends.
